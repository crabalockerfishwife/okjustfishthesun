About


ZPaths is here to help you with your everyday New Yorker needs. Upon entering our site, you are given multiple options to choose from for points of interest in the city: from subway entrances, to museums and galleries. We then return a heat map that displays the best areas in New York City to find those points of interest, as well as a bar graph displaying how common these ameneties are in each borough.

Created by Christina Ko, Justin Strauss, Fish Milnikiewicz, and Sunnam Quispe Stuyvesant High School Class of 2015 Mike Zamansky's Software Development Course Check us out on GitHub
